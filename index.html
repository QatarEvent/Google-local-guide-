Google Local Guide Competition Guide

Qatar Youth Hostels - Step by Step (Hindi Guide)

Step 1: Registration (15-22 April)

Hindi: Sabse pehle aapko competition ke liye register karna hoga.

Official website ya form link par jaakar apna naam, email aur details fill karein.

Example: Name: Imtiyaz Age: 20 Category: 18-39

Step 2: Google Local Guide Account Banayein

Hindi: Aapko Google Maps par Local Guide banna hoga.

Google Maps open karein → Profile → "Join Local Guides"

Example: Aap restaurant visit karte ho → review likhte ho → points milte hain

Step 3: Local Places Explore Karein

Hindi: Apne aas paas ke famous places visit karein.

  • Restaurants
  • Parks
  • Tourist places
Example: Souq Waqif visit karo Photos lo + experience likho

Step 4: Google Maps Par Upload Karein

Hindi: Photos aur reviews Google Maps par upload karein.

  • Photos add karein
  • Rating dein (1-5 stars)
  • Review likhein
Example Review: "Yeh jagah family ke liye perfect hai. Clean aur peaceful environment hai."

Step 5: Quality Content Banayein

Hindi: Achhi quality photos aur honest reviews likhein.

  • Clear photos
  • Detailed review
  • Helpful information
Example: Timing, price, parking info add karein

Step 6: Consistent Activity

Hindi: Regular uploads karein.

Jitna zyada aap contribute karoge utna zyada chance jeetne ka.

Example: Roz 1 new place upload karo

Step 7: Winners Selection

Hindi: Top 3 winners ko prize milega.

  • Best reviews
  • Most helpful content
  • Active participation

Important Tips

  • Fake review mat likho
  • Original photos hi upload karo
  • Consistent raho
